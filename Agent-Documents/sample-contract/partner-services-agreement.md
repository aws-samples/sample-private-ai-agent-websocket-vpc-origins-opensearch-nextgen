## 1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

Provider shall deliver cloud migration consulting services including assessment, planning, execution, and post-migration support for Company's on-premise infrastructure to AWS cloud environment.

### 1.1 Deliverables
- Infrastructure assessment report
- Migration plan document
- Execution of migration for up to 50 servers
- 30 days post-migration monitoring

### 1.2 Timeline
Work shall commence within 10 business days of contract execution and be completed within 6 months.
